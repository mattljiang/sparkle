# sparkle

- Step 1: Download the Sparkle extension. On this page, click Code, then Download ZIP. Unzip the folder on your computer.
<img width="1297" alt="Screen Shot 2020-12-25 at 5 15 24 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/9983876/103136184-10aeb300-4673-11eb-8f7c-460c0f3f0ccb.png">
- Step 2: Load the extension into Google Chrome. Navigate to chrome://extensions, click Load unpacked, then select the folder.
<img width="679" alt="Screen Shot 2020-12-25 at 5 16 45 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/9983876/103136087-39827880-4672-11eb-8ed3-9c1afc1aca97.png">
- Step 3: Pin the extension. At the top right corner of your browser window, click the Extensions puzzle icon and pin Sparkle.
<img width="339" alt="Screen Shot 2020-12-25 at 5 18 43 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/9983876/103136093-40a98680-4672-11eb-8e94-096f6aa9964a.png">
- Step 4: Go to the options page. Click on the Sparkle icon that just appeared and click on Options. 
<img width="282" alt="Screen Shot 2020-12-25 at 5 19 22 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/9983876/103136092-4010f000-4672-11eb-8d63-9bc2441d789c.png">
- Step 5: Set your identity. Choose who you are, then click save. 
<img width="814" alt="Screen Shot 2020-12-25 at 5 19 43 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/9983876/103136089-3be4d280-4672-11eb-9c97-1f74a78e005e.png">
- Step 6: Enjoy your new browsing experience!
